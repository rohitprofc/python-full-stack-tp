Golden Temple

The Golden Temple (Sri Harmandir Sahib), the holiest Sikh shrine, is a majestic structure in Amritsar, India. Built in the 1500s on a sacred pool, its golden dome and open design symbolize equality and inclusivity. A place of pilgrimage and peace, visitors can bathe in the pool, meditate, and experience Sikh culture.
